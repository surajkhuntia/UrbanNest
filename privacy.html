Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2026

Welcome to Urban Nest. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and in using our services. This Privacy Policy applies to our website and our modular housing services.

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, or otherwise contact us. The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us, the choices you make, and the features you use. This includes your name, email address, password, and configuration preferences.

We use personal information collected via our website for a variety of business purposes, including to provide, operate, and maintain our website; improve, personalize, and expand our website; understand and analyze how you use our website; and communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your data with third-party vendors, service providers, contractors or agents who perform services for us or on our behalf and require access to such information to do that work (e.g., payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting services).

4. Data Retention

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we collect from you, change that information, or delete it in some circumstances. To request to review, update, or delete your personal information, please submit a request to hello@urbannest.com.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Effective Date" and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible.
